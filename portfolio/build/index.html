I'm a creative Front End Developer and UI Designer from Kyiv, Ukraine. I build websites. I specialize in front-end development (HTML5, CSS3, Javascript etc), but sometimes I get my hands dirty with some back-end (php, MySQL).

I have many years experience as a front-end developer and graphic designer, creating responsive websites, layouts, and even make some videos. The work I provide is of highest quality, fully responsive, and tested in a wide range of devices.
